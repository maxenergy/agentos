# grill-requirements (v2)

## Purpose

Eliminate ambiguity before implementation.

## Modes

- /grill-feature
- /grill-bug
- /grill-design

## Required Questions

- What is the real goal?
- What is NOT the goal?
- What defines success?
- What defines failure?
- What assumptions may be wrong?
- What must NOT change?

## Output

- docs/goal/QUESTIONS.md
- docs/goal/ANSWERS.md
- docs/goal/ASSUMPTIONS.md
- docs/goal/UNKNOWN.md

## Rules

- No coding.
- No premature solutions.
- Surface all ambiguity.
